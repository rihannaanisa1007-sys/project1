    How to draw a Snake

    1. Draw a long, curved line for the body
    2. Draw a triangle for the head at one end of the line
    3. Add two small circles for the eyes on the head
    4. Add a forked tongue coming out of the mouth
    5. Add scales by drawing small, overlapping shapes along the body
