    • Capybaras

    Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world. They are semi-aquatic herbivores. They are known for their friendly nature and social behavior. They became extremely popular online due to their cute and calm demeanor.

    Things Capybaras Eat

    • Grass
    • Fruits
    • Vegetables

    How to draw a Capybara

    1. Draw a large oval for the body
    2. Draw a smaller oval for the head attached to the body
    3. Add two small circles for the ears on top of the head
    4. Add two small circles for the eyes and a small oval for the nose on the head
    5. Add four legs by drawing rectangles attached to the body and add details like toes and fur texture
